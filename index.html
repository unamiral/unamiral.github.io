Primitive CNN for Transit Detection

Detecting Transits with Convolutional Neural Networks

In this project, we explore the use of a primitive CNN algorithm to detect transits in light curves, which are periodic dips in brightness caused by planets passing in front of their host stars.

Future Work

Future improvements to the CNN algorithm could include incorporating more advanced architectures, such as recurrent neural networks (RNNs) or long short-term memory (LSTM) networks, to better capture temporal dependencies in the light curve data.
